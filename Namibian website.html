Namibian GIS Website

This website shows different types of maps of Namibia only. its aim is to share web maps that shows different spatial datas of Namibia . It contains a map of Karas schools which shows how schools are distributed in Karas Region. It also shows Conservancies of accross Namibia and the allocation of irrigation areas and water supply across Namibia.Furthermore this website shows schools in Namibia how are they distributed in the Namibia Regions. It also contains a static map showing transportation in Namibia.
